About Dr. Cheema

I am an Assistant Professor of Physics specializing in Experimental Condensed Matter Physics and Material Science. My Ph.D. research focused on the synthesis and characterization of modified Zinc Ferrite (ZnFe2O4) and Barium Titanate (BaTiO3) nanocomposites.

My work bridges fundamental physics and practical application, aiming to develop superior materials for Li-ion batteries, Supercapacitors, and Solid Oxide Fuel Cells (SOFCs). I utilize advanced characterization techniques like XRD and Impedance Spectroscopy to probe the atomic-level behavior of materials.
