Academic Profile

I am an Assistant Professor at Jaipur National University with a Ph.D. in Physics from Banasthali Vidyapith (2024). My research journey focuses on the intricate world of Experimental Condensed Matter Physics and Material Science.

Currently, I am investigating nanocomposites of Mn and Ni-doped Zinc Ferrite with Barium Titanate to achieve multi-ferroic properties. My goal is to develop advanced electrodes for next-generation Li-ion batteries and supercapacitors, driving the future of sustainable energy.
